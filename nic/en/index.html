NICOSIA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT VE

2024 Modern Architecture, Participatory Heritage, Immersive Reconstruction
Contribution Portal

The Nicosia International Airport Virtual Environment project is an extensive documentation and reconstruction of the lost Nicosia Airport. This project opens new avenues for public-engaged cultural heritage research, using the museum-exhibited virtual environment as a stage to collect and preserve memories. Through the immersive reconstruction, visitors can explore the airport as it once was, experience its unique architecture, and contribute their own stories and memories, creating a collective narrative of this significant historical site.
