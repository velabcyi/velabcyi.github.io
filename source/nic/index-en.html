Nicosia International Airport: VE

2024 A Digital Architectural Humanities Project
Contribution Portal

This installation is part of the Cyprus Institute's multidisciplinary and holistic effort to preserve the modern architectural heritage of the Nicosia International Airport. It features a virtual environment accessible through a point-and-click interface that allows the visitors to explore a digital reconstruction of the airport set in the late 1960s. Through spatially-distributed narratives, this playful interface enables visitors to interact with personas of the airport and view archival material from the prime years of its operation.

Visitors are warmly invited to contribute their stories, memories, and content (photos, etc.) to help the periodical enrichment of this virtual environment, so that we can create a more comprehensive, inclusive and diverse understanding of life at the airport at the time. Please help us preserve the collective memory of Nicosia International Airport by sharing your knowledge via the cabinet, online using the contribution portal, or by reaching out directly to the VELab. g.artopoulos@cyi.ac.cy
