This installation is part of the Cyprus Institute's efforts to preserve the modern heritage of the Nicosia International Airport. At our booth in the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation, visitors can explore a reconstruction of the airport, hear stories, and view archival material from its prime years of operation.

Visitors are warmly invited to contribute their own stories, photos, and videos to our archive. Through this outreach, are filling in picture of life at the airport at the time. You can make contributions at the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation, online using the contribution portal, or by reaching out directly to the VELab. g.artopoulos@cyi.ac.cy
