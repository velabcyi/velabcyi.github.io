Nicosia International Airport: VE

2024 A Digital Architectural Humanities Project
Send us stuffWorkshop, Nov 30th

The Cyprus Institute presents an immersive digital reconstruction of the Nicosia International Airport at the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation. Step into a virtual recreation of the airport, discover personal stories, and explore rare archival material.

Help us document the airport's role in shaping Cypriot modernity. Share your stories, photos, or videos at the Foundation, through our online portal, or by contacting the VELab at g.artopoulos@cyi.ac.cy. Your contribution will help us preserve the airport's place in Cyprus's collective memory.
