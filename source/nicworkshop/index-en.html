A Day at the Nicosia Airport

2024 SAT, NOV 30, 10AM Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation, Nicosia.
Register for the workshop

A captivating digital reconstruction of Nicosia International Airport as it existed in 1969 is on display at the "Cyprus Insula" exhibition in the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation. This showcase highlights the cultural and historical significance of this now-inaccessible site, which played a crucial role in modernizing the newly established Republic of Cyprus. It also celebrates the architectural legacy of this innovative yet abandoned structure.

As part of the exhibition's public events series, an interactive workshop will be held at the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation on Saturday, November 30 at 10:00 a.m. This event is open to all.

We invite you to immerse yourself in a virtual recreation of the airport, explore rare archival materials, and share your stories and memories of this remarkable place. Your participation will help us document the airport's pivotal role in shaping modern Cyprus. By contributing, you'll aid in preserving the airport's significance in Cyprus's collective memory.
