Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Virtual Environments Lab (VELab) at The Cyprus Institute collects and uses personal information in compliance with privacy laws. Our aim is to protect your privacy across all our participatory workshops, research, and software projects.

What We Collect

We collect personal information as needed for research and application development, including names, contact details, and text/media contributions.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information supports our research and the development of interactive softwares. Personal data is securely stored on premises or with services like Google Cloud.

Security

We implement standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access and data breaches. These include physical, electronic, and managerial protocols in accordance with GDPR and IRB.

Your Rights

You have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data under applicable laws. For any requests, please contact g.artopoulos@cyi.ac.cy.
