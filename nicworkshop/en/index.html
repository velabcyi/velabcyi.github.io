A Day at the Nicosia Airport

2024 SAT, NOV 30, 10AM Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation, Nicosia.
Sign up for Workshop

A captivating digital reconstruction of Nicosia International Airport as it was in 1969 is on display at the "Cyprus Insula" exhibition in the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation. This showcase highlights the cultural and historical significance of this now-inaccessible site, which played a crucial role in modernizing the newly established Republic of Cyprus.

As part of the exhibition's public events series, we are organizing an interactive workshop focused on collecting memories and materials related to the airport, while gathering feedback on our work. The workshop will be held at the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation on Saturday, November 30 at 10:00 a.m.

We invite those with personal memories, family stories, photographs, or historical materials to join us for group activities and exploration of the virtual environment. Can't make the workshop? The virtual environment is available during normal museum hours, and we may organize additional interactive tours later, using our signups as a point of contact.
