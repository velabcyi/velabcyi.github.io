Virtual Heritage Workshop: Nicosia International Airport

2024 Nov 30, 5PM-10PM Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation, Nicosia.
Sign Up

The Cyprus Institute invites you to a one-day immersive workshop showcasing new digital methods to study and preserve modern architectural heritage in Cyprus. The workshop will feature our virtual reconstruction of Nicosia International Airport, currently exhibited at the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation.

Objectives:

  • Present our work on the airport alongside existing cultural heritage efforts.
  • Gather feedback on the virtual reconstruction
  • Reflect on the airport and Cyprus' modern legacy
  • Explore future applications of virtual environments in heritage
