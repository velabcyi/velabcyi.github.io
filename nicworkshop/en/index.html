A Day at the Nicosia Airport

2024 SAT, NOV 30, 11AM Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation, Nicosia.
Sign up for Updates

A captivating digital reconstruction of Nicosia International Airport as it was in 1969 is on display at the "Cyprus Insula" exhibition in the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation. This showcase highlights the cultural and historical significance of this now-inaccessible site, which played a crucial role in modernizing the newly established Republic of Cyprus.

Our November 30th workshop at the Bank of Cyprus Cultural Foundation has reached capacity, with participants selected based on their contributions to our airport archives. However, we are gathering contact information for future interactive sessions as part of the BOCCF's ongoing public events series.

Add your name to our contact list for upcoming events and activities. In the meantime, you can explore the virtual environment during normal museum hours, and we'll keep you informed about additional interactive tours and events.
